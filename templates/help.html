How to Play Rick and Mordle

Objective of the Game

The goal of Rick and Mordle is to match a picture of a character with their name given their corresponding last seen location, origin, and episodes seen in. Test your knowledge of the show and try to achieve the highest score possible!

Notes

  • This is updated respective of season 5, and as of August 14th, 2024 the Rick and Morty API has not been updated further.
  • There may be errors with how the information is displayed, or what information is displayed.
  • Issues with this game are a result of the API. (There are 4 inputs for plain old Rick Sanchez for some reason)
